Valorie Curry

BIO

Valorie Mae Curry (born February 12, 1986) is an American actress. Her roles include the series Veronica Mars and the films The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn – Part 2 and Blair Witch. She starred on Fox's The Following and the PlayStation 3 tech demo Kara as the titular character, a role she reprised in the video game Detroit: Become Human.
